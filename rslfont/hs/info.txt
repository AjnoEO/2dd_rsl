selected: The selected finger(s) (index to pinky from 2 to 5; 0 if none)
closed, hook, forward, straight: Are there fingers with this positioning (-1 for neutral fingers, 1 for selected fingers)
dispersed: Are the fingers dispersed or touching each other side by side (0 if N/A)
thumb: The thumb positioning (0 = touching, 1 = over, 2 = forward, 3 = side, 5 = adjacent)
